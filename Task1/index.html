CATEGORIES

Fiction

Fiction is any creative work, chiefly any narrative work, portraying individuals, events, or places that are imaginary, or in ways that are imaginary.

Non-Fiction

Non-fiction is a broad genre of writing that encompasses all books that aren't rooted in a fictional narrative.

Self-Help

A self-help book is one that is written with the intention to instruct its readers on solving personal problems

Romance

A romance novel or romantic novel generally refers to a type of genre fiction novel which places its primary focus on the relationship and romantic love between two people.

Historical

Historical books usually contain the systematic study and documentation of the human past.

Horror

Horror is a genre that seeks to provoke fear, terror, shock, and other similar kinds of feelings in readers.
